Howzat

Know Your Legends

Cricket has always been a big part of my life — and Howzat is where that passion meets a daily challenge. It's a cricket guessing game that puts your knowledge of the sport's greatest players to the test: can you identify a legendary batter or bowler from their silhouette alone?

Each day brings a new challenge across two categories — bowler and batter — with a single guess to get it right. Compete on live leaderboards, track your win streak, and see how you stack up on the all-time hall of fame.

Daily Bowler & Batter Challenges
